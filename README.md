# Zomato Restaurant Analysis 

## Overview
Analysis of 9,542 restaurants from Zomato using Python and Pandas, exploring key business questions an investor would consider before opening a restaurant.

## Key Findings
- New Delhi is the most saturated market with 5,473 restaurants, while cities like Ahmedabad, Amritsar, Lucknow and Guwahati offer lower competition opportunities
- North Indian cuisine dominates with 3,960 restaurants, present in every top 10 most voted restaurant
- Restaurants in higher price ranges average significantly higher ratings (3.82 vs 2.00 for cheapest range)
- Restaurants offering online delivery rate 32% higher on average (3.25 vs 2.46)
- Bangalore claims 5 of the top 10 most voted restaurants despite having far fewer restaurants than Delhi
